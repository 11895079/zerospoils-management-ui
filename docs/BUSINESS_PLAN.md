# 🥑 ZeroSpoils Business Plan

**Prepared:** March 5, 2026  
**Company:** 11895079 Canada Inc.  
**Product:** ZeroSpoils — Mobile App for Household Food Waste Reduction

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**ZeroSpoils** is a cross-platform mobile app (iOS & Android) built with Flutter that helps households track food in their kitchen and prevent spoilage through smart inventory management and timely reminders. The app empowers users to **know what's in their kitchen, act before food spoils, and see the money they've saved**.

- **Stage:** Late development — MVP core loop complete, ~166 hours invested, ~80–107 hours to beta launch
- **Business Model:** Freemium (free MVP core + paid Pro tier ~$5/month or $40/year)
- **Target Market:** Busy parents, young professionals, and eco-conscious households in Canada
- **Unique Differentiator:** Offline-first architecture, privacy-by-design (no telemetry by default), gamified mascot experience ("Zesto the Avocado"), and OCR receipt scanning — **not** barcode-based like competitors

---

## 2. Problem Statement

### The Pain
- **~30% of groceries purchased by Canadian households are wasted** — that's roughly **$1,300+ per household per year** (NRDC estimates)
- Food rots in the back of fridges, duplicate purchases are made, and expiry dates are missed
- Existing solutions are either too complex, require constant manual input, or rely on barcode scanning (a saturated, low-differentiation approach)

### The Emotional Cost
- Guilt about wasting food while food prices soar in Canada
- Feeling disorganized and overwhelmed by kitchen management
- Wanting to be more sustainable but lacking practical tools

---

## 3. Solution — ZeroSpoils MVP

### Core Value Loop: **Add → Track → Remind → Save**

| Capability | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **🗂 Inventory Management** | Add items manually (or via OCR receipt scan), categorize by location (fridge/pantry/freezer), track quantities and expiry dates |
| **⏰ Smart Reminders** | Configurable local push notifications 1–5 days before expiry, delivered at user-selected times |
| **🔍 Priority Views** | "Expiring Soon" screen with bucketed urgency (Today / This Week / Expired) with one-tap actions |
| **🛒 Shopping List** | Create lists, convert expiring items, mark purchased items to automatically move to inventory |
| **📊 Waste Insights** | Track items consumed vs. wasted, see estimated money saved, earn badges for milestones |
| **🥑 Zesto Mascot** | Gamified companion that celebrates wins, offers storage tips, and encourages waste reduction habits |
| **🔒 Privacy-First** | All data local/encrypted, opt-in telemetry, CSV export, one-tap data deletion — PIPEDA/GDPR-ready |

## 6. Competitive Analysis

| Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | ZeroSpoils Advantage |
|-----------|-----------|------------|---------------------|
| **NoWaste** | Established, barcode scan | Complex UI, privacy concerns, cloud-dependent | Simpler UX, offline-first, privacy-by-design |
| **Fridgely** | Clean design | Limited features, no reminders | Full feature set + smart reminders |
| **Too Good To Go** | Large user base (B2C marketplace) | Not an inventory tool — different category | Complementary, not competitive |
| **Grocery Lists** (generic) | Simple, familiar | No expiry tracking, no waste insights | Integrated tracking + shopping + insights |
| **Spreadsheets / Notes** | Free, flexible | Manual, no reminders, no analytics | Automated reminders + gamification |

### ZeroSpoils Differentiation
1. **Privacy-first** — No account required, no cloud dependency, PIPEDA/GDPR-ready
2. **Offline-first** — Works in airplane mode, no latency
3. **Gamification** — Zesto mascot + badge system drives long-term engagement
4. **Photo/Receipt OCR** (Pro) — Differentiates vs. barcode-centric competitors
5. **Household sync** (Pro) — Family collaboration feature
