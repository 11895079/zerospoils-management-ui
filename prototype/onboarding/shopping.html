Shopping List Workflow

Planning a grocery run? Use the Shopping List tab!

📍 BEFORE you shop

✓ Check inventory first
(Do you need milk? Check if Jan 15 is coming up)
✓ Add to shopping list
(Things you actually need)

🏪 AFTER you shop

✓ Mark as purchased
(Check off the item from your list)
✓ Add to inventory
(Enter the expiry date)
↓
Item moves to 📦 Inventory tab

💡 This is how shopping list items become part of your inventory!
