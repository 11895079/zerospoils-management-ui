# 04 — Messaging Architecture

The messaging house. The roof is the one thing we want remembered; the pillars
are the three ways we prove it; the proof points are the evidence. Every channel
line in Part B must trace to a pillar here.

---

## 🏠 Roof — the core narrative

**One line:**
> ZeroSpoils remembers your groceries, so you don't waste them.

**One paragraph:**
> Good food goes to waste in busy homes — not from carelessness, but because
> it's easy to lose track. ZeroSpoils fixes that. Scan a barcode or a whole
> receipt and it logs what you bought, then quietly reminds you to use things
> before they're gone. Less money in the bin, less guilt, less waste — without
> changing how you live.

---

## Pillar 1 — 💰 Money saved (the lead)

**Message:** The food you forget is money you threw away. ZeroSpoils helps you
keep it.

**Supporting points:**
- The average household bins a meaningful chunk of its grocery budget each year.
  *(`{{STAT_HOUSEHOLD_WASTE_USD}}` — pending sourcing.)*
- Use-it-in-time nudges turn "forgotten and binned" into "eaten and enjoyed."
- See your wins add up — food saved, money kept.

**Proof points:** expiry alerts with adjustable lead time; a clear at-a-glance
view of what to use first.

---

## Pillar 2 — ⚡ Effortless (the closer)

**Message:** It does the remembering, so you don't have to.

**Supporting points:**
- Capture a whole shop in seconds — scan barcodes or a full receipt, no typing.
- Reads expiry and produce labels for you.
- Runs quietly in the background; no spreadsheets, no daily upkeep.

**Proof points:** barcode + full-receipt + fresh-produce OCR; offline-first so it
just works; Zesto's storage tips to make food last longer.

---

## Pillar 3 — 🌍 Better for the planet (the halo)

**Message:** Every meal you don't waste is a small win for the planet — and it
adds up.

**Supporting points:**
- Wasted food means wasted water, energy, and land — and needless landfill.
- A simple home habit is where the bigger change starts.
- *Use this pillar to deepen loyalty and earn sharing — never as the cold open.*

**Proof points:** cumulative "food saved" view; the brand-truth story in
`backstory.md` (the About layer).

---

## 🧱 Foundation

Sits on the approved positioning (`02`) and the voice rules (`03`). Spine:
**"ZeroSpoils remembers your groceries, so you don't waste them."**

---

## How to use this house

- **Cold / top-of-funnel** (App Store, ads, first-touch social): lead Pillar 1,
  support with Pillar 2. Planet stays implicit.
- **Mid-funnel** (onboarding, email): Pillars 1 + 2, introduce Pillar 3.
- **Loyalty / advocacy** (in-app wins, community, About): Pillar 3 comes forward.
- Never lead a stranger with Pillar 3. Never bury Pillar 1.
