# 03 — Brand Foundation

Who ZeroSpoils is, what it believes, and how it sounds. Built on the approved
positioning (`02`). This is the source of truth for *voice* — every channel kit
obeys the guide at the bottom.

---

## Purpose, vision, mission

**Purpose (why we exist):**
To help everyday households waste less of what they buy — keeping money in their
pockets and good food out of the bin.

**Vision (the world we want):**
A world where the food that comes into a home gets used, not forgotten — by
default, without anyone having to think hard about it.

**Mission (what we do about it):**
Make remembering effortless. Scan what you buy; ZeroSpoils does the remembering
and nudges you to use it while it's still good.

---

## Values

1. **On your side.** We fight forgetting — never blame the user. They're busy and
   human; we're the teammate, not the critic.
2. **Effortless by design.** We remove friction, never add it. If a feature (or a
   sentence) asks more of the user than it gives back, it's wrong.
3. **Honest.** Every claim is defensible; every byte of data is respected. No
   invented stats, no dark patterns, no guilt-selling.
4. **Warm, not worthy.** We're a helpful friend, not a cause. The planet matters
   deeply — and we win people by being likeable, not preachy.

---

## Personality

**Archetype:** Caregiver × Everyman — *"the friend who's quietly good with
money and food."*

Down-to-earth, generous, unpretentious, a little witty. Looks out for you
without making a fuss. Knows their stuff but never lectures. The opposite of a
finger-wagging eco-brand or a cold productivity tool.

If ZeroSpoils were a person at a dinner party: the one who notices you bought too
many avocados and says, *"here, let me show you a trick"* — not the one
quoting landfill statistics over dessert.

---

## Voice & Tone Guide

**Voice** (constant — who we always are):

| Attribute | Means | Sounds like |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| **Warm** | On the reader's side, human | "Heads up — that salmon's best used by Friday." |
| **Plain** | Short words, short sentences | "Scan it. We'll remember." |
| **Quietly witty** | A light touch, never trying too hard | "We do the remembering. You do the eating." |
| **Encouraging** | Progress over perfection | "Nice — that's three things saved this week." |

**Tone** (flexes by moment):

| Moment | Tone |
|--------|------|
| Onboarding | Welcoming, simple, confidence-building |
| Expiry nudge | Helpful, low-pressure, specific |
| A "win" (item used in time) | Warm, celebratory, brief |
| An error / empty state | Calm, reassuring, never alarmist |
| About / mission | Sincere — this is where the planet story lives |

---

### Do / Don't

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|-------|----------|
| "Stop good food going to waste." | "Reduce your household food waste footprint." |
| "Scan it. We'll handle the rest." | "Leverage automated inventory capture." |
| "That's about $40 saved this month." | "You are wasting too much food." |
| "Use it before Friday." | "URGENT: item expiring!!" |
| Lead with the moment / the money | Lead with the mission / the guilt |

---

### Lexicon

**Words we like:** use it, save, keep, remember, before it's gone, your fridge,
groceries, heads up, nice, worth, a few seconds.

**Words we avoid:** sustainability, footprint, leverage, optimize, utilize,
synergy, "reduce food waste" (as a headline), guilt, should, must, fail.

> We can *be* about sustainability without *saying* "sustainability" to a cold
> audience. Show the moment; skip the jargon.

---

### Before → After

1. **Before:** "ZeroSpoils helps you reduce household food waste through smart
   inventory management."
   **After:** "ZeroSpoils remembers what's in your fridge, so good food doesn't
   get forgotten."

2. **Before:** "You have 3 items expiring soon. Take action to avoid waste."
   **After:** "Three things to use this week — here's what's first."

3. **Before:** "Join the movement to fight climate change, one meal at a time."
   **After:** "Use what you bought. Keep the money. (The planet says thanks too.)"
