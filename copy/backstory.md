# ZeroSpoils — Our Story

Sometimes it may not feel like it, but humanity is so blessed.

We have the agency to shape our own fate — for the most part. Even when so many things are outside of our control, the most important thing remains in our hands: how we choose to show up.

There is an amazing resource, so abundant and so fecund that we have almost forgotten how extraordinary it is. We call it Earth. And alongside it we have been handed two gifts: the gift of will, and the gift of the world we live in.

In simple terms, we can decide how we want this world — or at least our part of it — to be.

This is why we created ZeroSpoils.

We want to keep this planet flourishing and beautiful for as long as we can. Every large goal starts with a small step. The step and habit we invite you to with this app is a simple one: reduce the food that goes to waste in your home.

Because it's not just about the food.

It's the energy spent growing it. The water used to raise it. The time it took to reach your table. The landfills quietly filling with what we didn't get to in time, making the environment that much harder for all of us to manage.

What we don't measure, we can't improve. And you don't rise to the level of your goals — you fall to the level of your systems.

ZeroSpoils is that system. A quiet, repeatable habit that turns intention into action, one item at a time. Scan what comes in. Know what needs to go first. Waste less. Live better.

We invite you to this revolution.

Because Earth is all we have — and it is far cheaper, far wiser, and far more beautiful to keep it running well.
