# ZeroSpoils — Our Story

Most food waste isn't careless.

It's a full fridge on a Sunday with no idea what expires Tuesday. It's herbs bought for one recipe. It's the chicken you were definitely going to cook on Thursday.

Not a character flaw. A systems problem.

So we built a system.

Scan what comes in. ZeroSpoils tracks expiry dates, surfaces what needs to go first, and keeps you quietly ahead of the waste — without the guilt trip.

Your wallet notices. The planet does too.

Because the food in your bin didn't just cost you money. It cost water, energy, and land to grow. And it ends up in a landfill that didn't need it.

One kitchen at a time, we can change that.

You don't rise to the level of your goals.
You fall to the level of your systems.

This is yours.

Welcome to ZeroSpoils.
