# In-App UX Copy

Product (in-app) copy, foundation-governed. Voice: warm, plain, no guilt.
Extracted from the retired `launch/launch-copy.md` and aligned to the locked
voice (`../foundation/03-brand-foundation.md`).

> This is *product* copy, not launch marketing — sequenced separately. Item
> placeholders use `{item}`.

---

## Onboarding (3 screens)

**Screen 1**
- **Headline:** Welcome to ZeroSpoils.
- **Body:** Everything you buy, remembered — so good food doesn't get forgotten.
- **CTA:** Get started

**Screen 2**
- **Headline:** Scan once. We'll remember.
- **Body:** Point your camera at a barcode or a whole receipt. We handle the rest — expiry dates, names, the lot.
- **CTA:** Show me how

**Screen 3**
- **Headline:** We remember, so you don't have to.
- **Body:** A gentle nudge before things expire — less food wasted, less money in the bin.
- **CTA:** Start saving

---

## Empty state (zero items)

> Nothing here yet.

## Expiry notifications

| Urgency | Copy |
|---------|------|
| 3 days out | "Heads up — {item} is best used in the next 3 days." |
| Tomorrow | "{item} expires tomorrow — worth planning tonight's meal around it." |
| Today | "{item} is best used today. A quick win before it's gone." |
| Overdue | "{item} may be past its best — worth a quick check." |

> Note: the old "use it or lose it" line was dropped to match the foundation
> (that idiom was retired in `06-naming-and-taglines.md`).
