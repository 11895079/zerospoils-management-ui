## Guardrails

- No "revolutionary," no big claims, no countdown. This phase is for learning.
- Ask for **one** thing: honest feedback (a reply, or the in-app feedback flow).
- Don't push for ratings here — the in-app prompt handles that after a real win
  (see `in-app-proof-capture.md`).
- Keep links as tokens; never paste a raw store URL inline.
