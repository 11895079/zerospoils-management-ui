## ASO notes

- Lead the **money** benefit in the first two lines (the part shown before
  "more"). Planet stays lower — halo, not hook.
- Subtitle/short-description carry the clearest keywords (expiry, food, save).
- Do **not** keyword-stuff the description; Apple ranks the keyword field, not
  the body. Keep the body human (Principle 3).
- Refine this listing after soft launch using real search/conversion data
  before the Phase 2 promotional push.
