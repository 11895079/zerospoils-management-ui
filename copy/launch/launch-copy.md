## App Store — Long Description [DRAFT]

Every week, a little bit of good food quietly disappears into the bin.
Not because you meant for it to — but because it was easy to lose track.

ZeroSpoils gives you back that track.

Scan groceries as they come in. The app reads expiry dates automatically and
reminds you what to use first, before it's too late. No spreadsheets. No
sticky notes. Just a calm, reliable system running in the background of your life.

**What you get:**
- Instant barcode scanning with auto-populated expiry dates
- OCR receipt scanning — add a whole shop in seconds
- Smart expiry alerts so nothing slips past you
- A running picture of what's in your home and what needs attention
- Per-item storage tips from Zesto, our built-in food guide

**Why it matters:**
Wasted food is wasted energy, water, time, and money. It's landfill that
didn't need to be there. ZeroSpoils won't change the world overnight — but
it will change your kitchen. And that is exactly where it starts.

You don't rise to the level of your goals.
You fall to the level of your systems.

This is your system.
